SECTION 1 — Refund Policy Overview

§1.1 Find Care Helper ("Find Care Helper," "we," "us," "our") is committed to customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be issued for Digital Products purchased through our website at findcarehelper.com (the "Site").

§1.2 Because Digital Products are delivered electronically and can be downloaded immediately upon purchase, all sales are generally considered final. However, we offer refunds under the specific circumstances described in this policy.

§1.3 This Refund Policy is incorporated into and forms part of our Terms of Service (/terms-of-service). Capitalized terms not defined herein shall have the meanings ascribed to them in the Terms of Service.

SECTION 2 — Eligibility for Refunds

§2.1 You may be eligible for a full refund if: (a) you request a refund within thirty (30) calendar days of the original purchase date; (b) the Digital Product is materially different from its description on the product listing page; (c) the Digital Product files are corrupted, incomplete, or cannot be opened with the software specified on the product listing page; or (d) you were charged multiple times for the same Order due to a technical error.

§2.2 Refund eligibility is determined at our sole discretion based on the merits of each individual request. We may request additional information or evidence to evaluate your refund claim.

SECTION 3 — Refund Process

§3.1 To request a refund, you must submit a written request to contact@findcarehelper.com that includes: (a) your full name as used during purchase; (b) the email address associated with your Account or Order; (c) the Order number or transaction reference; (d) the name of the Digital Product(s) for which you are requesting a refund; and (e) a detailed explanation of the reason for the refund request.

§3.2 We will acknowledge receipt of your refund request within two (2) business days and will process your request within seven (7) business days of receipt.

§3.3 If your refund request is approved, the refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please allow five to ten (5-10) business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your payment provider.

§3.4 Upon issuance of a refund, your License to the refunded Digital Product is immediately revoked. You must delete all copies of the refunded Digital Product from your devices and storage media. Continued use of a refunded Digital Product constitutes a violation of our Terms of Service.

SECTION 4 — Non-Refundable Circumstances

§4.1 Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances: (a) the refund request is submitted more than thirty (30) calendar days after the original purchase date; (b) you have substantially downloaded, used, or deployed the Digital Product in a project; (c) the Digital Product functions as described but does not meet your subjective expectations or personal preferences regarding style, aesthetics, or design choices; (d) you purchased the wrong product when the correct product was clearly described on the listing page; (e) you lack the software, hardware, or technical skill required to use the Digital Product, where such requirements were clearly stated on the product listing page; or (f) the product listing page accurately described the Digital Product's contents, format, and compatibility.

§4.2 Products purchased during promotional sales, with discount codes, or as part of bundles remain subject to this Refund Policy. The refund amount will reflect the actual price paid after any applicable discounts.

SECTION 5 — Duplicate Charges and Billing Errors

§5.1 If you believe you have been charged in error or have been charged multiple times for the same Order, please contact us immediately at contact@findcarehelper.com with your transaction details.

§5.2 Verified duplicate charges or billing errors will be refunded in full, regardless of the thirty (30) day refund window, within five (5) business days of verification.

SECTION 6 — Chargebacks

§6.1 We encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any payment disputes before initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card company. Filing a chargeback without first contacting us may result in the permanent suspension of your Account.

§6.2 If a chargeback is filed and subsequently found to be fraudulent or unjustified, we reserve the right to: (a) suspend or permanently terminate your Account; (b) revoke all Licenses associated with your Account; (c) pursue recovery of the disputed amount plus any chargeback fees incurred; and (d) report the incident to applicable fraud prevention services.

SECTION 7 — Exchanges

§7.1 We do not offer direct exchanges of Digital Products. If you wish to obtain a different Digital Product, you must request a refund for the original purchase (subject to the terms of this policy) and place a new Order for the desired product.
